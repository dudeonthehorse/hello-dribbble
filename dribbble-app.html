Dribbble’s Official iOS App

Greeting Dribbblers! It’s been a while since our last Dribbble newsletter—a year ago, yikes! We’ve been working on some exciting things in that time and we’ve finally launched one of the most requested features in the history of Dribbble—a mobile app! Our new Dribbble iOS app now available and free to download in the App Store.

A few months ago, we added all-star iOS developer Devin Ross to the team and he’s evolved his popular Balllin’ app as our official release. While we’ve been proud of our responsive web experience, nothing beats a native app for speed, performance, integration, and ease-of-use. Read more about the release on our blog.

We also have some exciting updates ahead that will include mobile uploads, activity and push notifications, more content from Dribbble, and Apple TV.
